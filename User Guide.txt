Here are some quick steps in order to run the program:

1. Download the zip file and extract it somewhere.

2. Open a terminal window in the extracted folder.

3. Type the command "./run" without the quotation marks.

4. Right-click to move the camera around.

5. Move the light by pressing on the w, a, s, or d keys.

6. Increase/decrease refractivity by pressing on the o or p keys.
